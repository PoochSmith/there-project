About

A collaboration between design curators Justine Boussard and Sarah Turner, There Project was founded to engage the design community in projects that help identify, promote and live societal values that advance a sustainable and fulfilling human existence.

How do we get from here to there? We believe in the power of design as a driver of change. We want to grow the community of designers using their skills and creativity to think about another way of living that is more sustainable and resilient, to design to encourage conviviality and regeneration of the planet and grow the appeal of a richer life with less.
